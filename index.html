Stories
Life in Frames
Mini-essays from my life: poverty, discipline, anime, rejection, obsession. The context behind the art.
Rudrapur • Early years
The boy who drew in the margins

While teachers chased marks, I chased shapes. Desks filled with rough sketches, notebooks with more drawings than answers. That was enough to start a quiet rebellion against a scripted life.

High school • Turning Point
The day “no” became fuel

A simple crush, a simple rejection—but it cracked something open. I realised I could either stay the nice, invisible boy, or I could rebuild myself. The gym, sketchbook and late-night walks became my temples.

Now • Act I
Why this website exists

This isn’t just a portfolio. It’s a checkpoint. A place where I can show my work, my scars and the philosophies shaping me into a top-tier creator.
