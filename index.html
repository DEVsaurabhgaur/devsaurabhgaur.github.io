About Me

I’m Saurabh Gaur from India – a self-taught artist and software developer. I didn’t come through art school or elite coaching. I come from curiosity, anime, late-night experiments, and a lot of trial and error.

My days are split between code, sketches, and reflection. I’m obsessed with how the inner world shapes the outer one – how our thoughts, discipline, and pain show up as lines, colours, projects, and decisions.
