Intro

I’m Saurabh Gaur, a self-taught artist and software developer from India. I grew up obsessed with anime, superheroes and science – now I turn that energy into portraits, concepts and code.

My world lives at the intersection of art, technology and mindset: cinematic portraits, anime-inspired pieces, and experiments with Python, AI and quantum.

This site is the home base of everything I’m building – visually, technically and personally.

Story

I didn’t come from art school or a big tech campus. I’m a first-generation creator who learned by experimenting, failing and trying again.

As a kid I was the one glued to cartoons, anime and science documentaries. That curiosity slowly turned into two parallel paths: drawing everything that inspired me, and learning how computers really work.

Today I’m building my own path as an artist, software developer and digital creator. My goal is simple: create work that looks good, feels true, and pushes me (and others) toward our highest potential.

SaurabhGaur.world is my hub – a place to track my growth, show my work, and connect with people who resonate with this journey.
