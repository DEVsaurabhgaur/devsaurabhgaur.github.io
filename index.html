Life Story in Chapters

From a small room full of anime and old textbooks to building my own visual universe. Here’s the short version.

Chapter 1 — The small room

Childhood · Bareilly

Cartoons, anime, Discovery Channel and a cheap phone with slow internet. No guidance, but a lot of imagination. This is where I started drawing on margins instead of notebooks.

Chapter 2 — Rejection & rebellion

Teenage years

Bullied for being poor and quiet. A crush, a rejection and a decision: either stay small or build myself from scratch. Art became the first weapon.

Chapter 3 — Digital canvas

Early 20s

Learned design, wallpapers, and the power of shipping work online. This site is the latest checkpoint, not the final form.
