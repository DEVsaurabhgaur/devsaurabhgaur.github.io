Life Story in Chapters

From a small room in Bareilly to building my own visual universe online. This is the short version of how the art started.

Chapter 1 — The small room

Childhood

Cartoons, anime, Discovery Channel and an old phone with slow internet. No guidance, no resources, just curiosity and empty pages to draw on.

Chapter 2 — Rejection & rebellion

Teenage years

Bullied for being poor and quiet. A crush, a rejection and a decision: either stay small or build myself from scratch. Art became the first weapon.

Chapter 3 — Digital canvas

Early twenties

Learned design, wallpapers, and the power of shipping work online. This site is not the final form – just one of the important checkpoints.
